Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book DJ Xoli S for my event?
+
For best availability, we recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and corporate events, and 1-3 months for club bookings. Peak season (November-February) books up quickly, so earlier inquiries are encouraged.
What is included in the booking fee?
+
The booking fee includes: DJ performance (2-6 hours depending on event), professional sound equipment (if needed), pre-event consultation, customized playlist based on your preferences, and travel within South Africa. Additional fees may apply for international events, special equipment requests, or extended performance times.
Can I request specific songs or genres?
+
Absolutely! We encourage clients to provide a list of must-play songs, preferred genres, and any songs to avoid. DJ Xoli S will work with you to create the perfect musical atmosphere for your event while maintaining her signature Afro House and electronic style.
What are the payment terms?
+
A 50% deposit is required to secure your date, with the remaining 50% due 7 days before the event. Payments can be made via bank transfer, credit card, or other agreed-upon methods. Corporate accounts may be eligible for different payment terms.
Do you travel for international events?
+
Yes, DJ Xoli S is available for international bookings. Additional costs for travel, accommodation, and work permits (if required) will be included in the quote. We have experience performing across Africa and Europe and can handle the logistics for international events.
