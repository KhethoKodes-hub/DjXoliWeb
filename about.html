Meet Xoli

DJ Xoli S is a trailblazing South African female DJ and music producer known for her unique fusion of Afro House, Electronic, and Deep House sounds.

Born and raised in Cape Town, Xoli discovered her passion for music at an early age, influenced by the rich musical heritage of South Africa and the vibrant sounds of the African diaspora.

Her journey began in underground clubs where she quickly gained recognition for her ability to read crowds and create unforgettable musical experiences. With each performance, Xoli blends traditional African rhythms with contemporary electronic elements, creating a sound that is both authentic and innovative.

Career Journey

2015

First Club Residency

Started her career with a weekly residency at "The Bassment" in Cape Town, developing her signature sound and building a loyal following.

2017

Breakthrough Performance

Headlined the "Afro Vibes Festival" in Johannesburg, gaining national recognition and securing bookings across South Africa.

2019

International Debut

First international performance at "We Love Africa" festival in Berlin, marking the beginning of her global journey.

2021

First EP Release

Released debut EP "African Skies" which received critical acclaim and expanded her reach to new audiences worldwide.

2023

Current Projects

Working on her first full-length album while touring internationally and mentoring young female DJs through her "She Decks" initiative.
