About

DJ Xoli S is a trailblazing force in South Africa's electronic music scene, bringing infectious Afro House rhythms and deep electronic beats to dance floors across the nation. With a passion for music that transcends boundaries, she creates unforgettable experiences that celebrate African culture while pushing the sonic boundaries of house music.

From intimate club nights in Cape Town to festival main stages, DJ Xoli S commands every crowd with her unique blend of technical skill, musical intuition, and pure energy. Her sets are a journey through sound, weaving together the pulsating heartbeat of Afro House with cutting-edge electronic production.
